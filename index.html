Course overview

This course introduces the conceptual foundations and mathematical language of quantum theory, with emphasis on physical understanding and systematic problem solving. Students are expected to have prior exposure to undergraduate quantum physics and to be comfortable with the basic Schrödinger equation, wavefunctions, and elementary operator methods.
